Experience / Projects

AllyBot: Robotic Assistance System for Human-Robot Interaction

Best Narrative Award - TEI 2024

Developed an interactive robotic system that uses natural language processing and computer vision to provide personalized assistance in educational environments. The system adapts to individual user needs and learning patterns, creating more engaging and effective educational experiences.

Python ROS Computer Vision NLP

Yachabot: Educational Robot with Conversational AI

Educational robot that employs conversational AI to adapt to individual student learning styles. The system provides personalized tutoring experiences and helps students develop critical thinking skills through interactive dialogue.

Python Machine Learning Conversational AI Educational Technology

PlantiBot: Towards the Design of a Robotic Plant for Mental Health Care

This study presents a robotic plant system that uses biomimicry and IoRT to offer therapeutic plant care experiences for university students, promoting mental well-being and awareness of native Peruvian flora in small living spaces.

IoT Robotics Agriculture Data Analytics
